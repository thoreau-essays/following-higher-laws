In this essay, we discuss Thoreau's revisions to the chapter titled "Higher Laws" in *Walden* as published in 1854. For evidence, we draw on the manuscript of *Walden* in the Huntington Library, HM 924.

We focus in particular on the digitized manuscript images in the gallery below. Clicking on any of these images will open it in a zoomable viewer.

Throughout the essay, you'll see details from the manuscript images. Clicking on a detail will take you to a larger view of it. Clicking on the associated thumbnail in the right margin will open a zoomable image of the entire manuscript page so that you can see the detail in context and explore the page.

Readers interested in searching the entirety of HM 924 should consult our [Manuscript Search Tool](https://digitalthoreau.org/manuscript-search-tool/) on the Digital Thoreau website.
