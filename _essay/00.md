In this essay, we discuss Thoreau's revisions to the chapter titled "Higher Laws" in *Walden* as published in 1854. For evidence, we draw on the manuscript of *Walden* in the Huntington Library, HM 924.

We focus in particular on the digitized manuscript images in the gallery below. Clicking on any of these images will open it in a zoomable viewer.
